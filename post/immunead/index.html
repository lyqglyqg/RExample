Single-Cell RNA Sequencing of Peripheral Blood Reveals Immune Cell Signatures in Alzheimer’s Disease

GEO data: GSE181279

Article: Single-Cell RNA Sequencing of Peripheral Blood Reveals Immune Cell Signatures in Alzheimer’s Disease

Journal: frontiers in Immunology

Abstract: The peripheral immune system is thought to affect the pathology of the central nervous system in Alzheimer’s disease (AD). However, current knowledge is inadequate for understanding the characteristics of peripheral immune cells in AD. This study aimed to explore the molecular basis of peripheral immune cells and the features of adaptive immune repertoire at a single cell level. We proﬁled 36,849 peripheral blood mononuclear cells from AD patients with amyloid-positive status and normal controls with amyloid- negative status by 5’ single-cell transcriptome and immune repertoire sequencing using the cell ranger standard analysis procedure. We revealed ﬁve immune cell subsets: CD4+ T cells, CD8+ T cells, B cells, natural killer cells, and monocytes–macrophages cells, and disentangled the characteristic alterations of cell subset proportion and gene expression patterns in AD. Thirty-one cell type-speciﬁc key genes, comprising abundant human leukocyte antigen genes, and multiple immune-related pathways were identiﬁed by protein–protein interaction network and pathway enrichment analysis. We also found high-frequency ampliﬁcation clonotypes in T and B cells and decreased diversity in T cells in AD. As clone ampliﬁcation suggested the activation of an adaptive immune response against speciﬁc antigens, we speculated that the peripheral adaptive immune response, especially mediated by T cells, may have a role in the pathogenesis of AD. This ﬁnding may also contribute to further research regarding disease mechanism and the development of immune-related biomarkers or therapy.

摘要：外周免疫系统被认为影响阿尔茨海默病（AD）的中枢神经病理。然而，目前的知识还不足以了解AD患者的外周免疫细胞的特征。本研究旨在探索外周免疫细胞的分子基础和适应性免疫库的特征。我们通过5‘单细胞转录组和免疫库测序，分析了36849个外周血单核细胞(来自淀粉样蛋白阳性AD患者和阴性患者)。我们发现了5个免疫细胞亚群： CD4+ T细胞、CD8+ T细胞、B细胞、自然杀伤细胞和单核-巨噬细胞，并揭示了AD中细胞亚群比例和基因表达模式的特征性改变。通过蛋白-蛋白相互作用网络和通路富集分析，鉴定出31个细胞类型特异性的关键基因，其中包括丰富的人类白细胞抗原基因和多个免疫相关通路。我们还发现在T和B细胞中的高频扩增克隆子，在AD中T细胞多样性降低。由于克隆扩增表明激活了针对特定抗原的适应性免疫反应，我们推测外周适应性免疫反应更可能由T细胞介导的，可能在AD的发病机制中起作用。这一发现有助于进一步研究疾病机制和免疫相关生物标志物或治疗的发展。
